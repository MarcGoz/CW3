About the Indigenous People's Rights Campaign

The Indigenous People's Rights Campaign is an initiative that aims to protect and promote the rights of indigenous peoples around the world. Our goal is to create awareness, promote education, and advocate for the protection of their cultures, traditions, and languages. We believe that every individual, regardless of their ethnicity, has the right to self-determination, and the right to preserve and protect their culture and identity.
